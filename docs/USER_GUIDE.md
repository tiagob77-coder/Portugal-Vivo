## Introduction
Welcome to the Portugal Vivo User Guide! This document provides detailed step-by-step instructions to help you effectively use the app and explore the rich cultural heritage of Portugal.

## Installation
1. Download the Portugal Vivo app from the App Store or Google Play.
   - **For iOS:** [App Store Link]
   - **For Android:** [Google Play Link]
2. Open the app after installation.
3. Follow the on-screen instructions to create an account or log in if you already have one.

## Main Navigation
- The main navigation menu is located at the bottom of the screen and includes the following tabs:
  - **Home:** Overview of features
  - **Heritage:** Explore various heritage sites
  - **Maps:** Access interactive maps
  - **Route Planning:** Plan your journeys
  - **Favorites:** Save your favorite locations
  - **Settings:** App configurations

## Exploring Heritage
1. Tap on the **Heritage** tab.
2. Browse through categories such as historical sites, museums, and landmarks.
3. Select a site to view details, including images and descriptions.
4. Example Screenshot: [Add screenshot of the heritage screen]

## Maps
- The **Maps** feature allows you to view heritage locations on the map.
1. Tap on the **Maps** tab.
2. Zoom in or out to explore different areas.
3. Click on map markers to see information about each location.

## Route Planning
1. Go to the **Route Planning** tab.
2. Input your start and destination addresses.
3. Choose from available transportation options: walking, driving, cycling.
4. View your route on the map along with estimated travel time.

## Favorites
- To add a location to your favorites:
1. Navigate to the location overview.
2. Tap the star icon at the top right.
3. Access saved favorites from the **Favorites** tab to view them later.

## Gamification
- Earn points and badges by visiting various heritage sites. 
1. Check your progress in the **Gamification** section.
2. Share achievements on social media to encourage others to explore.

## AI Narratives
- Experience rich narratives powered by AI that provide context and stories about heritage sites.
1. Click on the **AI Narratives** section in the app.
2. Select a site to listen to its story narrated by AI.

## Community Contributions
- Engage with other users by submitting your reviews and photos.
1. Go to a heritage site page and click the **Contribute** button.
2. Upload your content and share your experiences.

## Settings
1. Access the **Settings** tab to customize your app experience. 
2. Change notification preferences, language, and account information.

## Troubleshooting
- If you encounter issues:
  - Ensure that you have the latest version of the app.
  - Check your internet connection.
  - Restart the app if it becomes unresponsive.

For further assistance, contact our support team via the app or email us at support@portugalvivo.com.
